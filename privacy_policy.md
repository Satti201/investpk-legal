## 2. What This App Does

InvestPK allows users to manually record, track, and analyse their personal investment portfolio including PSX stocks, mutual funds, and pension funds. All data entered into the app is personal financial data that you provide voluntarily for your own tracking purposes.

---

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3a. Data You Provide
- Google account name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
- Profile photo URL from your Google account
- Investment transaction records you manually enter (fund names, amounts, dates, units, tax paid)
- Watchlist items you add
- SIP reminders you create
- Dividend records you enter
- Notes you attach to transactions

### 3b. Data Collected Automatically
- App crash reports and error logs (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- App usage analytics (via Firebase Analytics, if enabled)
- Device type and operating system version

### 3c. Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your bank account details
- We do not collect your brokerage login credentials
- We do not access your actual investment accounts
- We do not collect payment information
- We do not collect your CNIC or any government identification
- We do not read your contacts, messages, or call logs
- We do not access your camera or microphone

---

## 4. How We Use Your Data

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Google name and email | To identify your account and display your profile |
| Investment records | To show you your portfolio dashboard, holdings, and analytics |
| Crash reports | To fix bugs and improve app stability |
| Usage analytics | To understand which features are used and improve the app |

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your data for any purpose other than providing the InvestPK service to you.

---

## 5. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database service provided by Google LLC. Firebase servers used are located in the United States. By using InvestPK you consent to your data being transferred to and stored in the United States under Google's data protection standards.

Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

---

## 6. Data Security

- All data is transmitted over HTTPS encrypted connections
- Firebase Firestore security rules ensure each user can only access their own data
- We do not store passwords — authentication is handled entirely by Google Sign-In
- We do not have access to your raw password at any point

---

## 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party except:

- **Google Firebase** — for data storage and authentication (as described above)
- **Legal requirements** — if required by Pakistani law or court order

We do not share data with any financial institutions, brokers, or investment platforms.

---

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — all your data is visible to you within the app
- **Export** your data — you can view all transactions within the app
- **Delete** your data — use the Delete Account option in Profile > Settings, or email us
- **Correct** your data — edit or delete any transaction within the app

### Account Deletion
To permanently delete your account and all associated data:
1. Open InvestPK → Profile → Settings → Delete Account
2. Or email mansoorsatti2498@gmail.com with subject "Delete My Account"

We will delete all your data from Firebase within 30 days of your request.
